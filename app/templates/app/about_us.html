about Josh

I am a certified meditation and yoga instructor and have experienced the benefits of a dedicated practice. I hope to share these teachings to help bring greater awareness and calm to busy lives.

My teaching style is influenced by yoga philosophy and focuses on bringing attention into the present moment through bodily sensations. This type of practice allows us to experience a greater awareness of our body and mind, release tension, and promote the development of mindful habits in our daily life.

These mindfulness-based techniques are simple and effective when practiced consistently over time. Through dedicated practice, we build greater patience and acceptance, which help us to reshape the way we react to everyday stress and frustration. Quite naturally, we become calmer, happier, and more balanced in all aspects of our life.

formal training

I received my Meditation Instructor Certification from University of Holistic Theology. My primary teacher for yogic meditation is Dennis Young.

I completed my Vinyasa Yoga Teacher Training at Down Under School of Yoga and have studied Vinyasa Yoga with Peter Crowley, Barbara Benagh and Natasha Rizopolous as well as Yin Yoga with Josh Summers.
