elderly care




Mindfulness is not limited to a certain group of people, age, race or gender - these practices are helpful in reducing stress for everyone but often are not accessible to the whole community. In an age where loneliness is on the rise, a recent study of older adults showed that practicing meditation on a consistent basis reduced those feelings of loneliness. Research has also shown that meditation can reduce inflammation, improve psychological wellbeing, and increase cognition in older adults.
