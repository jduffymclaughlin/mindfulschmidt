In an age of emphasizing office culture, offering yoga and meditation as a benefit can help attract new talent, increase the productivity of your team, and reduce the stress of your employees. Guided Meditation

These sessions are designed to introduce and reinforce mindful practices and explore the benefits of a routine practice. The first session acts as an introduction to discuss fundamental mindfulness exercises and include a guided meditation. Recurring sessions will build upon material explored in previous classes and incorporate discussion about our experiences in meditation as well as daily life.
