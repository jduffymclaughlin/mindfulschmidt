schools




Yoga and meditation practices are effective in reducing stress and anxiety in kids and adults. Research has shown that students who consistently practiced mindfulness exercises reduced their anxiety and boosted working memory. Another study on social-emotional development showed students who engaged in mindfulness exercises reported greater empathy, perspective-taking ability, emotional control, and optimism, and showed a decrease in symptoms of depression and aggression.

meditation for teens

In these sessions we explore the idea of mindfulness in an age of technology as well as the daily pressures of being a young adult. We will discuss group and individual stresses we experience as young people and will be guided through fundamental mindfulness exercises to reduce stress and promote comfortability of self.

guided meditation

These sessions are designed to introduce and reinforce mindful practices and explore the benefits of a routine practice. The first session will act as an introduction to discuss fundamental mindfulness exercises and include a guided meditation. Recurring sessions will build upon material explored in previous classes and incorporate discussion about our experiences in meditation as well as daily life.

flow yoga*

These 30-minute classes promote and reinforce a daily stretching practice throughout the workday. Great for office breaks as no change of clothes is needed.
